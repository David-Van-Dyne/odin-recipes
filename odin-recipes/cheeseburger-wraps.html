Cheeseburger Wraps Recipe


Description

Cheeseburger wraps are a fun and flavorful twist on the classic cheeseburger, combining all the savory ingredients you love into a convenient handheld form. These wraps typically feature seasoned ground beef cooked to perfection, topped with melted cheese, and layered with classic burger fixings such as lettuce, tomato, pickles, and onions. Everything is then wrapped in a soft flour tortilla, making it easy to enjoy without the mess of a traditional burger bun. They can be grilled, pan-seared, or even baked for a warm, crispy finish that enhances the texture and flavor.

Perfect for a quick lunch or weeknight dinner, cheeseburger wraps offer both comfort and convenience. They’re highly customizable—you can add bacon, special sauce, or swap in turkey or plant-based meat for a healthier alternative. Pairing them with fries, chips, or a fresh salad makes for a balanced and satisfying meal. Whether you’re feeding a crowd or just craving something hearty and portable, cheeseburger wraps bring the best of both worlds: the taste of a juicy cheeseburger with the ease of a wrap.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup chopped lettuce
  • 1/4 cup pickles, sliced
  • 1/4 cup ketchup
  • 1/4 cup mustard

Steps

  1. In a skillet, cook ground beef over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
  2. Add taco seasoning and a little water, simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Warm tortillas in the microwave or on a skillet.
  4. Assemble wraps: layer beef, cheese, tomatoes, lettuce, pickles, ketchup, and mustard on each tortilla.
  5. Roll up the tortillas tightly and serve immediately.
