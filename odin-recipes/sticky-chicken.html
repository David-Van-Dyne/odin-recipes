Sticky Chicken Recipe

Description

Breaded sticky chicken is a mouthwatering dish that combines crispy, golden-brown chicken with a sweet and savory glaze that clings to every bite. The chicken pieces are first coated in a seasoned breading—often made with flour, cornstarch, and spices—then fried or baked until perfectly crunchy. Once cooked, they’re tossed in a rich, glossy sauce made from ingredients like honey, soy sauce, brown sugar, and garlic, creating that irresistible sticky coating that balances sweetness with a hint of tang. The result is a dish that’s both crispy and saucy, offering a satisfying texture and bold flavor in every bite.

Perfect for weeknight dinners or entertaining guests, breaded sticky chicken pairs beautifully with steamed rice, noodles, or fresh vegetables. Its restaurant-quality flavor and simple preparation make it a favorite for home cooks looking to impress. The sticky glaze caramelizes slightly as it cools, locking in the flavor and giving the chicken a shiny, appetizing finish. Whether served as a main dish or appetizer, this recipe delivers the perfect combination of crunch, sweetness, and umami that keeps everyone coming back for more.

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1/2 cup ketchup
  • 1/4 cup worcestershire sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp ginger, grated
  • flour
  • 4 eggs
  • breadcrumbs
  • olive oil

Instructions

  1. Using the flour, eggs, and bread crumbs, bread the chicken breasts.
  2. In a bowl, mix soy sauce, honey, garlic, ketchup, worcestershire sauce, and ginger.
  3. Heat olive oil, cook the breaded chicken until golden brown.
  4. Bring the sauce to a simmer, then add the chicken, cook for about 30 seconds on each side, coating it well.
  5. Serve with rice and vegetables.
